How It Works

Finding your perfect off-campus home is easy with HomeMore

1

Search & Filter

Enter your preferences: budget, number of bedrooms, distance from campus, and more. Our smart filters help you find exactly what you're looking for in seconds.

2

Compare Options

Add properties to your comparison list and view them side-by-side. See pricing differences, amenities, landlord ratings, and distance calculations all in one place.

3

Read Reviews

Check verified student reviews about the property condition, landlord responsiveness, and overall living experience from people who've been there.

4

Connect & Move In

Message landlords directly through our platform, schedule viewings, and complete your rental application. We're here to support you every step of the way.
